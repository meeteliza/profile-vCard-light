About me

I'm an English major at Beijing Foreign Studies University's School of English and International Studies, where I earned my bachelor's degree in 2021 and where I'm on track to complete my master's degree in 2024. Over the years, I've completed a total of xx courses, xx presentations, and xx term papers in English. I make sure to maximize my learning experience and therefore I'm experienced in critical thinking, adept information gathering, and effective self-expression. My presentations and term papers can be found in the portfolio section.

Unlike many English majors who choose literature as their field of interest, mine is in political science and international relations. My master thesis delves into the relationship between sanction target's regime type and sanction success rate. My bachelor thesis deals with Trump administration's maximum pressure campaign toward Iran. On top of that, I earned my second bachelor major in Diplomacy at School of International Relation and Diplomacy in 2021, which offered me additional systematic knowledge of this field. This academic path has allowed me to explore the complex dynamics that shape our global community, including the power of perceptions (constructivism), the process involved in (foreign policy) decision making, the (quantitative) methods with which we can make inference about the real world, and many more.

Outside of academia, I'm passionate about graphic design, video editing, web development and python automation. currently I only manage to master the first two skills, which enables me to blend my love for visual aesthetics with my academic pursuit, enlarging my boundary of effective communication I relish the opportunity to convey messages through the power of design. I believe that the marriage of words and visuals can create a profound impact, and I'm excited to continue exploring this synergy in my academic and personal projects. check out my design projects here.
